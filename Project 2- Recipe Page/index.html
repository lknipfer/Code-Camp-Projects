Skyline Dip

Nothing beats the taste of fresh skyline dip at the restaurant. But this one comes pretty close! Follow this simple, easy to make recipe to satisfy your skyline dip cravings that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • medium sized microwavable bowl
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 10 oz can of Skyline Chili
  • shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Spread 8 ounces of cream cheese into glass 8.5 x 6.5" pan
  3. Pour skyline chili into medium sized microwavable bowl and microwave for 1 minute
  4. Pour skyline chili over the cream cheese layer
  5. PLace pan in preheated oven for 10 minutes
  6. Remove from oven, top with shredded cheddar cheese
  7. Let stand for 5 minutes and serve with tortilla chips
